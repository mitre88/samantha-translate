# Samantha Translate

Subtitle: Real-time voice translator

Promotional Text:
Hear speech in another language and listen to the translation in yours.

Description:
Samantha Translate is a simple, premium voice translator for conversations, travel, interviews, and daily listening.

Open the app, choose your output language, start listening, and Samantha speaks the translation in real time. The app auto-detects the source language, so you do not need to set up a language pair before every conversation.

Privacy is simple: Samantha Translate does not save your audio, transcripts, or translation history. Audio is processed live to provide the translation.

Subscription:
New subscribers can start with a 3-day free trial. After the trial, Samantha Translate renews weekly at $4.99 unless cancelled at least 24 hours before renewal. You can manage or cancel your subscription in your Apple account settings.
