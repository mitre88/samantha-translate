# Samantha Translate Design Direction

## Product Promise

Samantha Translate is a premium real-time voice translator for interviews, travel, and live conversations. The app should feel quiet, fast, private, and native to iOS.

## Research Notes

Refero style search for voice and Apple-adjacent interfaces highlighted these useful patterns:

- Apple AirPods Pro: white canvas, polished glass, restrained typography, product object as the hero.
- Voicenotes: voice-product identity built from a simple mark, canvas white, slate ink, and light cyan/green signal colors.
- Voiceflow: AI product clarity using strong blue accents against neutral UI.
- Tapbots Ivory: dark-mode confidence with graphite surfaces and simple native controls.

## Visual System

- Core mark: white voice orb, black waveform, cyan signal edge.
- Backgrounds: native grouped system backgrounds in the app; dark/near-black only for marketing and icon contrast.
- Accent: cyan for voice signal, soft green for active/live translation.
- Typography: system semantic styles only, with bold reserved for state and action labels.
- Layout: one clear primary action, compact status, language control, then live transcript.
- Motion: pulse only while listening or during splash. Respect Reduce Motion.

## App Store Assets

- Icon must remain text-free and recognizable at 60px.
- Screenshots should lead with the live translation promise, then interview/travel use, subtitles plus spoken output, Apple subscription clarity, language choice, and privacy.
- Marketing art can use generated backgrounds, but must never fake app evidence or unsupported claims.
